StorytellerContent is a Boise based multimedia and entertainment production company. As a small band of driven creative thinkers, it is our mission to conceptualize and develop communication between storytellers and their audiences. We aim to provide an environment that is accessible, adaptable, and that instills change on a multitude of artistic platforms. Our studio space allows us a range of services in both commercial and narrative domains. We produce professional quality tales, from theatre performance and short films to documentaries and commercials. Let us help you through the creative process so you can tell your story.

Launching in May 2016, Studio 208 is a multi-purpose space that provides creatives in film, photography, theater, comedy and other performance art the resources to manifest versatile content within their given trade.

Jason Caldwell is the co founder of StorytellerContent, a death star production company once believed to be sent to earth to destroy all other production companies. Jason worked in the hotel industry for nine years and hated almost every second of it. It has been an amazing rollercoaster since he decided to move back to Idaho. Whether he is acting in short movies and commercials or filming and editing, Jason will always be first in the kraft service line. He currently lives on the out skirts of Boise where you can find him riding his road bike through someone’s garden or break dancing in the streets.
